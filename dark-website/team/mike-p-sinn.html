About me

Hi! I'm a software engineer dedicated to accelerating the development of cures for chronic diseases, dementia, and mental illness. My work focuses on leveraging decentralized clinical trials, AI-driven research, and policy reform to remove bottlenecks in medical innovation.

Mission

To create a civilization free of unnecessary suffering by optimizing public policy, decentralizing clinical trials, and leveraging AI to make cures more accessible, faster, and cheaper.

Stuff I'm Working On

  • Cure Acceleration Act – A global framework to reform public policy, incentivizing rapid cure development.
  • Decentralized FDA (dFDA) – An open-source, automated clinical trial platform to remove regulatory bottlenecks.
  • Right to Trial – Ensuring patients have the legal right to access and contribute data on experimental treatments.
  • Wishonia – A decentralized system for problem-solving and collective intelligence.
  • QuantiModo – A decentralized autonomous organization to coordinate humanity to win a global war on disease

What I Believe

  • Health innovation should move at the speed of software. Bureaucracy should not slow down life-saving research.
  • Decentralization unlocks progress. Open collaboration and transparency are key to solving big problems.
  • AI and automation can optimize everything. From policy drafting to trial execution, technology can remove inefficiencies.

Let’s work together to eliminate unnecessary suffering and bring cures to the world, faster!
